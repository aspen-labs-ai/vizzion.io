## Recommended Strategic Positioning

### New Positioning Statement:

**For growth-focused roofing contractors who waste time on unqualified leads,**  
**Vizzion is the revenue platform that turns website visitors into pre-sold homeowners**  
**by letting them visualize their new roof before you even meet.**

**Unlike traditional quote forms that attract tire-kickers,**  
**Vizzion captures emails before showing results, ensuring every lead is genuinely interested.**

**The result: 3x more qualified leads and 60% higher close rates in your first 90 days.**

---

## Key Differentiators to Emphasize

1. **Email capture BEFORE visualization** (unique mechanic)
2. **Roofing specialization** (not generic horizontal tool)
3. **Platform, not widget** (ecosystem, not point solution)
4. **Built by contractors** (authentic origin story)
5. **Pre-qualification system** (lead quality vs. lead quantity)

---

## Brand Voice & Personality

### Current: Generic professional tech company
### Recommended: Contractor-savvy revenue partner

**Voice Traits:**
- Straight-shooting (no corporate jargon)
- Results-obsessed (always tie to ROI)
- Contractor-savvy (use industry terms naturally)
- Encouraging (not expert-lecturing)

**Example Rewrite:**

**Before:**

**After:**
> "Stop wasting time on tire-kickers. Vizzion shows homeowners their new roof only AFTER they give you their email. If they're not willing to share contact info, they were never serious buyers anyway."
